THE PROBLEM WITH PORTFOLIOS

I’ve clicked through a lot of portfolios—some are visually stunning but quickly forgotten, while others are packed with so much text that they become overwhelming.

Conversations with recruiters and designers made it obvious: what really matters in a portfolio isn’t surface-level polish or endless explanations, but clear proof of real-world problem solving and skills.

That’s when I realized my own portfolio needed to be interactive, memorable, and truly showcase my problem solving approach as well as my design and programming skills. –
CLARIFYING THE ISSUE

Core Issue

The core problem? Most portfolio examples are pretty on the outside yet hollow on the inside. You see eye-catching project images, but when you actually click through, there’s little about the creator’s real role—or you’ll see a long list of skills without any real evidence of how those skills were applied.

I set out to design a site from scratch where users could actively highlight information, interact with different UI elements, and even generate message drafts with AI—making every visit unique. My target audience: recruiters seeking proof of ability, fellow designers looking for inspiration, and future-me tracking my own progress.

Success Measure

For me, success means every visitor leaves not just impressed, but with a true, clear understanding of what I can bring to the table.

VISUAL EXPLORATION - LONG IMAGES

I approached the website design as an ongoing experiment—building a variety of layouts, integrating 3D effects, and layering in interactive touches like highlighters, carousels, and chatbot features. Every detail, from micro-animations to color modes, was chosen to reward curiosity and give each page its own personality, without losing sight of the big picture. The result is a site that feels cohesive yet playful, encouraging visitors to explore and interact rather than just scroll and forget.

Key Features & Technical Implementation

  • Interactive Highlighter: Developed custom vanilla JS modules for a personalized content exploration experience.
  • AI-Powered Features: Integrated the OpenAI API for a smart chatbot and contact message drafting based on user highlights.
  • Advanced Animation: Used GSAP for fluid micro-animations and page transitions, and Three.js for engaging real-time 3D effects.
  • Challenges: The main challenge was learning advanced animation libraries from scratch and integrating multiple interactive elements without sacrificing site speed or performance.
