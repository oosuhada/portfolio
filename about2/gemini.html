Hi, I'm Oosu.

In Korean, my name "Oosu" carries multiple meanings:
to help and to guard (佑守),
to be excellent (優秀),
and to feel deeply (憂愁).

These meanings reflect how I approach design —
with purpose, excellency, and empathy.

With a background in data analysis, frontend development, and entrepreneurship, I create elegant, human-centered digital experiences that balance aesthetics with usability, while always keeping the bigger picture in sight.

AND NOW SOME FUN FACTS ABOUT MYSELF

Name-Guided Path

I'm a believer in old wisdoms, especially the idea that we grow into our names. Mine certainly guided me to where I am today.

Certified Sensory Player

I'm certified in aroma candle-making, perfumery, and wine sommelier – all about crafting sensory experiences. I bring that same approach to digital experiences.

Piano Precision Mindset

My parents got rid of my piano so I wouldn't become a pianist. Still, that precision and obsession with detail now shape my design process.

Holistic Experience Design

I used to run a wine bar. Branding, service, and sensory experience, all designed by me. It taught me design isn't just visual – it's holistic.

Curiosity-Driven Journey

I've moved across fashion, data, wine, and design fields – chasing curiosity, but always grounded in understanding people and crafting better experiences for them.

Resilient Creative Journey

I got bullied after I ranked #1 in art class among the boys. That pushed me away from art for a while. But in the long run, the detour gave me broader life experience – so no hard feelings.
