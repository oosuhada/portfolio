Hi, I'm Oosu.

In Korean, my name "Oosu" carries multiple meanings:
to help and to guard (佑守), to be excellent (優秀), and to feel deeply (憂愁).

These meanings reflect how I approach design — with purpose, excellency, and empathy.

With a background in data analysis, frontend development, and entrepreneurship, I create elegant, human-centered digital experiences that balance aesthetics with usability, while always keeping the bigger picture in sight.

AND NOW SOME FUN FACTS ABOUT MYSELF

Click on each dot to reveal a fun fact.

Name-Guided Path

I'm a believer in old wisdoms, especially the idea that we grow into our names. Mine certainly guided me to where I am today.

Certified Sensory Player

I'm certified in candle-making, perfumery, and as a wine sommelier— all disciplines focused on crafting sensory experiences. I bring this same multi-sensory approach to designing digital products.

Piano Precision Mindset

My parents once got rid of my piano so I wouldn't become a pianist. Despite their efforts, that early training in precision and obsession with detail now fundamentally shapes my design process.

Holistic Experience Design

I used to run a wine bar where I designed everything: the branding, the service flow, and the sensory atmosphere. It taught me that great design isn't just visual—it's a holistic, immersive experience.

Curiosity-Driven Journey

My career has moved across fashion, data, wine, and now design, a journey driven purely by curiosity. The common thread has always been understanding people and crafting better experiences for them.

Resilient Creative Journey

I was once bullied after ranking #1 in art class. That pushed me away from a creative path for a while. But the detour gave me broader life experience, making me a more resilient designer today. So, no hard feelings.
